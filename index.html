Introduction

Hi there! My name is Emily Mackay.

My love of programming began as a child, when I built my first website at the age of 12. As an adult I spent seven years working in the dental industry before re-visiting an old passion and enrolling in college to study computer science. Now, I am soon-to-be-graduated with an Associate Degree of Science majoring in Computer Science from Langara College.

Through my educational journey, I have gained experience in full-stack web development, database design, and software design, with a growing passion for front-end development and UX/UI design. My full list of skills can be viewed in my Portfolio and Education sections below.

Outside of school, I enjoy visiting BC's beautiful backcountry by foot or on skis, as well as listening to music and attending shows to support local and international talent.
